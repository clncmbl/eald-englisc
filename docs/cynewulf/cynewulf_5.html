Ond þā onġeat se cyning þæt ond hē on þā duru ēode ond þā unhēanlīċe hine werede oþ hē on þone æþeling lōcude, ond þā ūt rǣsde on hine ond hine miċlum ġewundode.
  • Þā onġeat se cyning þæt.
  • Cynewulf on þā duru ēode.
  • Þā Cynewulf unhēanlīċe hine werede.
  • Cynewulf lōcude on þone æþeling.
  • Cynewulf hine werede oþ hē on Cyneheard lōcude.
  • Cynewulf ūt rǣsde on Cyneheard.
  • Cynewulf miċlum ġewundode Cyneheard.
